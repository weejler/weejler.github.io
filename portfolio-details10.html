My Weekly Contributions

In much the same fashion as prior design projects there were a variety of weekly objectives to complete involving meeting minutes, design reviews, and extra meetings. For the meeting minutes I was responsible for generating ideas and then overseeing sketches related to a glove prototype which would end up becoming the main aspect of our project. Soon after the design reviews began in which I helped take notes on our prototype regarding improvements suggested by faculty members and TAs which were then subsequently implemented. I also attended every weekly meeting which was important to keep progressing since at one point half our group was out sick. During this period I acted to keep work on track and we suceeded in finishing all our work well ahead of the assigned due dates each week.
