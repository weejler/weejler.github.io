Wireless Music Player (Personal)

Maximum Power Point Tracking Algorithm Implementation

The MPPT Challenge

Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) is crucial for efficient solar power systems. This project focused on developing a highly efficient and fast Perturb and Observe (P&O) algorithm implementation to dynamically adjust the system impedance and maximize power harvest under changing environmental conditions.

Software & Efficiency

  • **Algorithm:** Custom P&O implementation optimized for high-frequency switching.
  • **Hardware:** Utilized a high-speed DSP (Digital Signal Processor) to handle the complex calculations required for tracking.
  • **Power Conversion:** Designed a buck converter stage with low-Rds(on) MOSFETs and optimized dead-time control.
  • **Efficiency Gain:** Achieved a verified system-level efficiency of 97.5%, exceeding the performance of many commercial PWM controllers by over 20%.
