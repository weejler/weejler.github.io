Detailed Case Study: Low-Power Industrial Monitoring

Project Overview

This project involved designing a compact, highly reliable device for real-time monitoring of current and voltage in commercial power distribution panels. The primary challenge was achieving high data acquisition accuracy (0.1% margin of error) while maintaining exceptionally low power consumption for long-term deployment.

Technical Implementation

  • **Microcontroller:** Utilized the STM32L4 series for its advanced low-power modes and robust peripheral set.
  • **Design Focus:** 4-layer PCB design to minimize noise and optimize power planes.
  • **Data Transmission:** Implemented a LoRaWAN module for long-range, low-bandwidth data transmission to a central server.
  • **Key Achievement:** Reduced average power draw to 10ÂµA during sleep, enabling years of operation on a single battery pack.
