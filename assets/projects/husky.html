Role and Responsibilities

As a designer for the **Production Engineering Team at Husky Technologies**, my primary responsibility was providing detailed mechanical and electrical support for the design and manufacturing of custom **injection molding machines (IMM)**. This role was critical in ensuring the final machine configuration met both internal production standards and stringent client specifications, particularly for Watercom LTD.

Image: Detailed CAD assembly of an IMM clamping unit (Conceptual).

Key Contributions

My work involved extensive use of **Siemens NX and AutoCAD** for machine layout, part detailing, and generating blueprints for the manufacturing floor. Specific contributions included:

  • **Mechanical Design:** Modifying and detailing large-scale machine components (e.g., platens, injection units) to accommodate specialized tooling and utilities.
  • **Electrical Support:** Generating and reviewing electrical schematics for proper sensor, heater, and motor integration based on machine modifications.
  • **Tolerance Analysis:** Conducting critical tolerance stack-up analysis to guarantee component fit and machine longevity under high stress.
