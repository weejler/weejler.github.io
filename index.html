I'm Eli Taylor, a Mechatronics and Biomedical Engineer specializing in machine learning algorithms and low-power embedded systems for use in implantable brain-computer interface contexts through HADILAB at McMaster University. With a passion for creating efficient, innovative hardware, I thrive on taking complex concepts from an initial design to a finalized, tested circuit. My experience ranges from designing machines for industrial applications to investigating dedicated hardware for efficient template matching and neuronal spike sorting.
